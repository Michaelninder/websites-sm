About Me

I'm a passionate Laravel developer with a deep love for crafting elegant web solutions. With years of experience in full-stack development, I specialize in building scalable, maintainable applications that solve real-world problems.

As the owner of XPsystems.eu and europehost.eu, I bring both technical expertise and business acumen to every project. I also created mtex.dev, a platform dedicated to creating free, open-source websites for developers using Laravel PHP.

My work spans from enterprise applications to creative web experiences. I believe in modern development practices, clean code architecture, and delivering exceptional user experiences. Check out my portfolios at fternis.de and michaelninder.de to see my latest work.
